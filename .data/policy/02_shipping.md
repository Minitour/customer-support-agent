# Shipping Policy

## Shipping Zones

ShopEase ships to the following zones:

| Zone | Countries | Estimated Delivery |
|------|-----------|-------------------|
| Domestic | United States | 3–5 business days |
| Canada | Canada | 5–8 business days |
| Europe | UK, EU member states | 7–14 business days |
| Asia-Pacific | Australia, Japan, Singapore, South Korea | 10–20 business days |
| Rest of World | All other countries | 14–30 business days |

## Shipping Carriers

We partner with the following carriers:

- **DHL** — international and domestic express
- **FedEx** — domestic and international
- **UPS** — domestic and international
- **USPS** — domestic economy
- **Amazon Logistics** — select domestic zip codes

The carrier is assigned automatically at the time of shipment.

## Shipping Costs

- **Free standard shipping** on all orders over **$50** within the United States.
- Orders under $50 incur a flat **$4.99** domestic shipping fee.
- International shipping rates are calculated at checkout based on destination and package weight.

## Tracking Your Order

Once your order ships, you will receive an email with a tracking number and carrier name. You can also view real-time tracking information by logging in and visiting your Orders page.

## Expedited Shipping

Expedited (1–2 business day) shipping is available for domestic orders at an additional cost, calculated at checkout.

## Address Issues

If your package is undeliverable due to an incorrect address, it will be returned to our warehouse. A reshipping fee may apply. Please double-check your shipping address before placing an order.

# Privacy & Data Policy

## Data We Collect

ShopEase collects the following types of data:

- **Account information**: name, email address, password (stored hashed)
- **Order information**: items purchased, shipping address, payment method (last 4 digits only)
- **Usage data**: pages visited, search queries, device type (used to improve our service)

## How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:
- Process and fulfill orders
- Send order confirmation and shipping notifications
- Provide customer support
- Improve our platform and personalize recommendations

We do **not** sell your personal data to third parties.

## Data Retention

- Account data is retained as long as your account is active.
- Order data is retained for 7 years for legal and tax purposes.
- You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

## Cookies

ShopEase uses cookies for session management, analytics, and personalization. You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings.

## Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:
- Access the personal data we hold about you
- Correct inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your data (right to be forgotten)
- Opt out of marketing communications

To exercise these rights, contact privacy@shopease.com.

## Security

All data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest. Access to personal data is restricted to authorized personnel only.

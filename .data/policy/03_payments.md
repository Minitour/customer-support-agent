# Payment Methods

## Accepted Payment Methods

ShopEase accepts the following payment methods:

### Credit & Debit Cards
- Visa
- Mastercard
- American Express
- Discover

### Digital Wallets
- Apple Pay
- Google Pay
- PayPal

### Buy Now, Pay Later
- Klarna (available for orders $30–$2,000)
- Afterpay (available for orders $35–$2,000)

### Store Credit
Store credit (issued for returns of sale items or via promotions) can be applied at checkout and combined with other payment methods.

## Payment Security

All transactions on ShopEase are encrypted using **TLS 1.3** and processed through PCI-DSS Level 1 compliant payment gateways. We do not store full card numbers on our servers.

## Currency

All prices are displayed and charged in **US Dollars (USD)** unless otherwise specified. International customers may see additional currency conversion fees from their bank.

## When Are You Charged?

Your payment method is charged at the time your order is **confirmed and placed**, not when it ships. For Buy Now Pay Later options, the payment schedule is determined by the BNPL provider.

## Failed Payments

If your payment fails, your order will not be placed. Please check:
- That your billing address matches what your bank has on file.
- That you have sufficient funds or credit.
- That your card has not expired.

Contact your bank if the issue persists, or try an alternative payment method.

## Invoices & Receipts

A digital receipt is emailed automatically after each purchase. You can also download invoices from the Orders section of your account.

# Return Policy

## Standard Returns

ShopEase offers a **30-day return window** for most items purchased on our platform. To be eligible for a return, items must be:

- In their original, unused condition
- In the original packaging with all tags attached
- Accompanied by the original receipt or proof of purchase

## Sale Items

Items purchased **on sale or at a discounted price** (including during promotional events such as Black Friday, Cyber Monday, or seasonal clearance sales) are eligible for **store credit only**. Cash or card refunds are **not available** for sale items.

- Sale items must still be returned within 30 days of purchase.
- Store credit will be issued within 5 business days of receiving the returned item.
- Store credit does not expire and can be used on any future purchase.

## Non-Returnable Items

The following items **cannot be returned**:

- Perishable goods (food, flowers, newspapers, magazines)
- Personal care items (cosmetics, skincare, opened hygiene products)
- Downloadable software products
- Gift cards
- Items marked as "Final Sale"

## Defective or Damaged Items

If you receive a defective or damaged item, contact us within **7 days of delivery**. We will arrange a free return and offer a full refund or a replacement, at your discretion.

## How to Initiate a Return

1. Log in to your account and navigate to your Orders page.
2. Select the order and click "Request Return."
3. Follow the instructions to print a prepaid return label.
4. Drop off the package at any authorized carrier location.

Refunds are processed within **5–7 business days** of receiving the returned item.

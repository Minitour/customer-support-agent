# Contact & Escalation

## How to Reach Us

ShopEase offers multiple support channels:

### Live Chat (AI Agent)
Available 24/7 via the chat widget on our website. The AI agent can answer policy questions and look up your order status instantly.

### Human Support Team
Available Monday–Friday, 9 AM–6 PM EST.

- **Email**: support@shopease.com (response within 24 hours)
- **Phone**: 1-800-SHOPEASE (1-800-746-7327), Monday–Friday 9 AM–6 PM EST
- **Live Chat with Human**: Available during business hours via the chat widget (click "Talk to a person")

## When Will I Be Escalated to a Human?

The AI support agent will automatically escalate your conversation to a human agent when:

- Your request requires performing an action (e.g. processing a refund, modifying an order, cancelling a subscription)
- The AI cannot find a relevant answer in its knowledge base
- You express frustration or explicitly request a human agent

When escalated, a human agent will reach out to you via email within **4 business hours**.

## Common Escalation Scenarios

These requests **require a human agent** and cannot be handled by the AI:

- Requesting a refund for a specific order
- Cancelling a pending subscription charge
- Disputing a charge or payment
- Reporting fraud on your account
- Requesting account deletion
- Complaints or formal feedback

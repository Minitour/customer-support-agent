# Warranty Policy

## Manufacturer's Warranty

Most products sold on ShopEase come with a **manufacturer's warranty**. The warranty period and coverage vary by product and brand. Warranty information is listed on each product's detail page.

Common warranty durations:
- Electronics & smartphones: 1–2 years
- Laptops & tablets: 1 year
- Appliances: 1–5 years (varies by brand)
- Clothing & apparel: No manufacturer warranty (see return policy)
- Beauty & skincare: No manufacturer warranty (subject to return policy)
- Jewelry: 6 months against manufacturing defects

## What Is Covered

Manufacturer warranties typically cover:
- Defects in materials or workmanship
- Hardware failures under normal use

## What Is NOT Covered

Warranties do not cover:
- Physical damage (drops, cracks, spills)
- Damage caused by misuse or unauthorized modifications
- Normal wear and tear
- Cosmetic damage (scratches, dents)
- Accessories and consumables (batteries, cables after 90 days)

## How to Make a Warranty Claim

1. Contact ShopEase support with your order number and a description of the defect.
2. Our team will verify your purchase and check warranty eligibility.
3. We will connect you with the manufacturer or arrange a replacement or repair as applicable.
4. In some cases, you may be asked to ship the product to the manufacturer's service center.

## ShopEase Extended Warranty

ShopEase does **not** currently offer its own extended warranty program. We recommend purchasing extended warranties directly from manufacturers where available.

## Out-of-Warranty Repairs

For products outside the warranty period, we can connect you with authorized repair centers. Repair costs are the customer's responsibility.

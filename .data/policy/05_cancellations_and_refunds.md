# Cancellations & Refunds

## Order Cancellation

You may cancel an order **before it ships** directly from your account's Orders page by clicking "Cancel Order." Once an order has shipped, it can no longer be cancelled — please initiate a return instead.

**Eligibility for cancellation:**
- Orders in "Placed" or "Processing" status can be cancelled.
- Orders with status "Out for Delivery" or "Delivered" cannot be cancelled.

## Refund Timeline

| Method | Timeline |
|--------|----------|
| Credit / Debit card | 5–10 business days |
| PayPal | 3–5 business days |
| Apple Pay / Google Pay | 5–10 business days |
| Store credit | Instant (upon confirmation) |

Refunds are issued to the original payment method. If the original payment method is no longer valid, please contact support.

## Partial Refunds

Partial refunds may be issued in the following situations:
- Only part of an order is returned.
- Items are returned outside the 30-day window (at ShopEase's discretion).
- Items show signs of use but are not defective.

## Non-Refundable Fees

Shipping fees are **non-refundable** unless the return is due to our error (wrong item shipped, defective product).

## Subscription Cancellations

ShopEase **does not currently offer subscription products**. If you have a question about a recurring charge, please contact our support team to investigate.

## Dispute Resolution

If you believe a charge is incorrect, contact our support team within 60 days. We will investigate and respond within 5 business days. If unresolved, you may escalate through your payment provider.
